God of War

Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
Launch Date: April 20, 2018
Genre: Action, Hack and Slash
Rating: 94/100 (Metacritic)

God of War reimagines the iconic series with a deeper narrative, focusing on the journey of Kratos and his son Atreus through Norse mythology. Blending brutal combat with emotional storytelling, players experience a transformation of Kratos from a god of vengeance to a father seeking redemption. The game features breathtaking visuals, innovative mechanics like the Leviathan Axe, and memorable encounters with gods and monsters alike.
