Cyberpunk 2077

Publisher: CD Projekt
Launch Date: December 10, 2020
Genre: Action RPG, Open World
Rating: 86/100 (Metacritic - after patches)

Cyberpunk 2077 immerses players into the neon-lit dystopian future of Night City, a megacity obsessed with power, glamor, and body modification. You play as V, a mercenary with cybernetic enhancements, navigating corporate intrigue, gang warfare, and moral dilemmas. Despite its rocky launch, updates and the Phantom Liberty expansion have elevated the game into a critically praised RPG with deep storytelling, customization, and stunning visuals.
