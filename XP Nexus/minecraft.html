Minecraft

Publisher: Mojang Studios
Launch Date: November 18, 2011
Genre: Sandbox, Survival
Rating: 93/100 (Metacritic)

Minecraft is a sandbox game that allows players to build, explore, and survive in procedurally generated worlds made entirely of blocks. Whether crafting structures, mining for resources, or fighting off creatures, players can express unlimited creativity and adventure. With modes ranging from creative to hardcore survival, and thriving community mods and servers, Minecraft continues to evolve as one of the most beloved and influential games of all time.
