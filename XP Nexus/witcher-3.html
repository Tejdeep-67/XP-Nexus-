The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Publisher: CD Projekt
Launch Date: May 19, 2015
Genre: Action RPG, Open World
Rating: 93/100 (Metacritic)

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a critically acclaimed open-world RPG that follows Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter known as a Witcher, in his quest to find his adopted daughter. Set in a dark fantasy world filled with complex characters and morally grey choices, the game offers rich storytelling, impactful side quests, and breathtaking environments. With its massive expansions and mod support, The Witcher 3 has become a defining RPG of the decade.
