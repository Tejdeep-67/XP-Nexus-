Forza Horizon 5

Publisher: Xbox Game Studios
Launch Date: November 9, 2021
Genre: Racing, Open World
Rating: 92/100 (Metacritic)

Forza Horizon 5 delivers the ultimate open-world racing experience set in the vibrant landscapes of Mexico. With dynamic seasons, weather effects, and hundreds of vehicles to choose from, the game offers adrenaline-pumping freedom on roads, deserts, jungles, and mountains. Praised for its breathtaking visuals, fluid gameplay, and massive content updates, Forza Horizon 5 is a crown jewel in the racing genre and a visual showcase for next-gen consoles.
