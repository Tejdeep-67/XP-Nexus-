Grand Theft Auto V

Publisher: Rockstar Games
Launch Date: September 17, 2013
Genre: Action-Adventure, Open World
Rating: 97/100 (Metacritic)

Set in the sprawling fictional state of San Andreas, Grand Theft Auto V follows the intertwined stories of three protagonists—Michael, Franklin, and Trevor—as they engage in high-stakes heists and navigate a dangerous world of crime. The game offers players unmatched freedom, a dynamic and richly detailed open world, and a satirical narrative that reflects modern society. With its cinematic presentation and vast online multiplayer component (GTA Online), GTA V remains one of the most influential and best-selling games in history.
