Ghost of Tsushima

Publisher: Sony Interactive Entertainment
Launch Date: July 17, 2020
Genre: Action-Adventure, Stealth
Rating: 83/100 (Metacritic)

Ghost of Tsushima places players in the shoes of Jin Sakai, a samurai warrior defending his homeland against the Mongol invasion of Tsushima Island. With a rich, cinematic presentation and fluid sword combat, the game blends historical inspiration with artistic beauty. Players can explore a vast open world, master stealth and combat techniques, and choose between the path of honor or the way of the Ghost. It's a celebration of Japanese culture and samurai legend.
