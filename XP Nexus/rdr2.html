Red Dead Redemption 2

Publisher: Rockstar Games
Launch Date: October 26, 2018
Genre: Action-Adventure, Western
Rating: 96/100 (Metacritic)

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an epic tale of life in America at the dawn of the modern age. Set in 1899, players step into the boots of Arthur Morgan, an outlaw and member of the Van der Linde gang, as he navigates survival, loyalty, and the collapse of the Wild West. With stunning landscapes, immersive storytelling, and a richly detailed open world, RDR2 is a benchmark in narrative-driven gaming. The game also features Red Dead Online, adding a multiplayer frontier experience.
