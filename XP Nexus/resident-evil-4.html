Resident Evil 4

Publisher: Capcom
Launch Date: March 24, 2023 (Remake)
Genre: Survival Horror, Action
Rating: 93/100 (Metacritic)

The Resident Evil 4 Remake brings the survival horror classic into the modern era with reimagined visuals, improved mechanics, and deeper storytelling. Players control Leon S. Kennedy as he investigates a mysterious European village while rescuing the president’s daughter. With intense action, atmospheric design, and grotesque enemies, the game reaffirms itself as a defining entry in the Resident Evil franchise and a must-play for horror fans.
