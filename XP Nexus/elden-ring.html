Elden Ring

Publisher: Bandai Namco Entertainment
Launch Date: February 25, 2022
Genre: Action RPG, Open World
Rating: 96/100 (Metacritic)

Elden Ring is a genre-defining action RPG developed by FromSoftware and directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki, with worldbuilding contributions from George R. R. Martin. Set in the mystical Lands Between, players explore a vast, interconnected open world filled with cryptic lore, towering bosses, and deep customization. Praised for its difficulty, design, and freedom of exploration, Elden Ring is hailed as a masterpiece in modern gaming and winner of numerous Game of the Year awards.
